Visual Design Artifacts

Artifact 1#

In this project I had to take a picture of myself, then choose multiple colors from White, gray, then all the way to black in order to create depth as I traced over the painting with these colors as shown.

Artifact 2#

In this project it was dedicated to Black artists that we had to study over and create a biography of their life. We showcased their work, made our own pages on them and even in real life pocket books to carry. After we finished we presented them in a group and shared our mini pocketbooks consisting of 8 pages stemming from your indivisuality and creativity on how you based this artist.

Artifact 3#

We were taught about how to create shoes and how to design them, in this project we designed our own shoes. In this I incoorporated blue and light green tones with a Black bottom and blue Laces.

Artifact 4#

This is my favorite project of them all, in this 3D dimensional Block I made I made a sunset on top contrasting it with dark concrete streets and noncolorful buildings. I added many small details such as Graffiti with my Middle name Initial L, little statelites on top of the buildings, crosswalks, and a street sign.

Artifact 5#

In this project we had to create a row of patterns and colors then multiply them all across the screen as duplicates of each other. I did not know what kind of colors I wanted so I simply decided on picking all of them sort of like a rainbow. Then I noticed that all the shapes looked like faces so I made them all indivisually different faces and personalities. So then I combined the words Rainbow Face.
