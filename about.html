Growing up

I was born in the winter during the year 2007 to a Dominican/Puerto Rican household that never slept. Growing up I was always a very shy and introverted child that was pretty much sheltered up until middle school hit. I never really liked school and was bullied up until the end of elementary also adding to my dislike in it. Up until 7th grade when covid hit I began to speak more and express myself because of some pages on tiktok informing me how to be more confident not to say I was never confident as a kid but the idea of being confident wasnt really nessacary if I didnt have any insecurities or knew what to be confident about. It wasnt until 9th grade hit where I completely changed and developed into a new person from being that shy introvet to the loud extrovert that wasnt scared to talk to anyone.

Likes
